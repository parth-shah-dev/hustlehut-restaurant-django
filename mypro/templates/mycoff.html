Black coffee is a classic beverage enjoyed worldwide for its bold and robust flavor. Made from freshly ground coffee beans brewed with hot water, it offers a rich and aromatic experience without any added milk or sugar. Known for its simplicity and purity, black coffee is cherished by coffee enthusiasts for its natural and intense taste profile.

Roasted coffee, on the other hand, is a broader term that encompasses all types of coffee beans that have undergone the roasting process. Whether it's a light, medium, or dark roast, all coffee beans are roasted to some extent to develop their flavors and aromas.
Dark roast coffee refers to coffee beans that have been roasted for a longer duration, resulting in a darker color and stronger flavor profile. The extended roasting process brings out bold and intense flavors, often with notes of chocolate, caramel, and smokiness. Dark roast coffee typically has a lower acidity level compared to lighter roasts, resulting in a smoother and richer taste.
